Welcome to Geo-HQ! This website is all about geography and offers fun and informative activities such as a quiz and a fun facts page. You can find everything this app has to offer by viewing each entity in the navigation bar. This website was made as a special project for a course in software development.

Under the "About" tab, you will find important information regarding the author of this website, ways in which you may contact him, links to his professional platforms, and much more. Under the "API" tab, you will find a link to the API utilized in this website and details about the API including what it is being used for and how it is referenced. Under the "Quiz" tab, you will find a geography quiz made especially for those avid quiz-takers and ever-learning minds. Last, under the "Fun Facts" tab, you will find a way in which you can see a plethora of fun facts about any given country, all by just typing in a nation's name.
